Data Science Salaries

Do you know your worth?

Given the volatility of the economy and tech job market, particularly in recent years, it's important to stay informed of any changes and fluctuations in compensation. This data visualization tool is intended to give you, the data scientist, insight into what the state of the market is and how market rate has fluctuated in the last couple years (2021-2023). You can filter by country, year, experience level, company size, remote/hybrid/on-site, etc. In our "Breakdown by Skill" tab, you can also see which data science skills have the greatest impact on salary.

To get started, click on a country in the tree map below!
