Analysis of Time Trends

This page focuses on the break-down of salary time trend for Data Scientists from three perspectives (Country, Experience Level, Company Size). It will consist two parts. The first part will look at the timely trend from country perspective and the second part will look at the trend from Experience Level as well as Company Size perspective individually and interactively via hover cross-filter.

Time Trend by Country

This section focuses on the checking the yearly trend for each country. The color represent different countries. In addition, it is pre-filtered to the countries with over 50 jobs reported in total to keep it neat. The size of the square represent the volume of job reported. The user can be filtered by countries to see the trend of a specific country or leave the countries as “All” to compare the timely trend for all major countries.

Time Trend by Experience & Company Size

These graphs will be able to check time trend of DS salary by Experience Level and Company Size both individually and interactively via hovering cross-filter. The colors represent different countries and Company Size accordingly on each visualization. The size of the squares represent the volume of job reported. You can filter the countries to see the trend of a specific country and the default value was set to “US” + “CA”
